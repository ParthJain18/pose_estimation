For Age Group: 11-15 Years:

1. Discover basic table tennis techniques and rules.
2. Foster enthusiasm and love for the sport.
3. Participate in skill-building training sessions.
4. Receive personalized coaching from mentors.
5. Embrace teamwork, discipline, and fair play.
6. Compete in local tournaments and friendly matches.
7. Explore the history and culture of table tennis.

Age Group: 16-18 Years:

1. Master advanced table tennis strategies and techniques.
2. Develop agility, speed, and mental focus.
3. Compete in regional and national competitions.
4. Cultivate a competitive mindset and sportsmanship.
5. Specialize in specific playing styles and tactics.
6. Apply sports science principles for peak performance.
7. Prepare for professional and competitive challenges.

Age Group: 18+ Years:

1. Engage in elite-level table tennis coaching programs.
2. Pursue professional opportunities and career pathways.
3. Demonstrate leadership and sportsmanship qualities.
4. Represent in international tournaments and events.
5. Connect with top players and coaches for mentorship.
6. Develop mental resilience and overcome challenges.
7. Achieve excellence in table tennis and personal growth.
