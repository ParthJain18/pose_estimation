India's Sporting Tapestry: A Rich Legacy

In India, sports have ancient roots, evident in traditional games like kabaddi and kho kho. Influences from colonial rule introduced modern sports such as cricket and football, which quickly gained popularity. Throughout history, sports have been integral to Indian culture, fostering camaraderie and competitive spirit. Today, India boasts a diverse sporting landscape, reflecting a rich heritage of athleticism and passion.
